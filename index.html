    About Us

    OneHome seeks to contribute to the developmental sector by facilitating engagement of the volunteering community, building capacity, and creating value added services for NGOs.

    Vision

    OneHome aims to be the biggest platform for organizational entities and individuals to work in synergy towards social value creation. It aims to enable new forms of information collection, processing, and broadcasting that were never possible, and help NGOs achieve their goals.

    Mission

    OneHome aims to provide comprehensive resource solutions for the social sector by creating symbiosis amongst NGOS and volunteers, and adding value and structure to these associations.
